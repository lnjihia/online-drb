About Driver Recruitment Bureau

Driver Recruitment Services is an Industrial Recruitment Agency specialising in the supply of permanent, contract and temporary drivers, from 3.5 Tonne Van to Category C + E Articulated Goods Vehicle. The company is based in Boldon at the top of the A19 corridor, ideally placed to service the North of England. Our customers range from Family run Businesses, National Hauliers, to large National Blue Chip logistics providers.


The success of the business has been largely based upon our experienced and expert knowledge from our staff members. With a combined total of 40 years experience in recruitment and training within the transport and logistics specialism, we have been able to achieve and establish major client business and relations. We are proud to state that we are able to serve our customers with the highest quality and reliability in mind. Our services can be tailored to provide whatever the client requires, whether a driver for one day, right through to providing an entire recruitment campaign on behalf of a client.


Our staff have exceptional knowledge and experience, gained dealing with customers within the transport and logistics environment. We understand that our customers require a fast turnaround and an agency who is reliable and can also provide a responsive and professional service; DRS is able to provide this alongside experience and market knowledge of the new laws, legislation and compliance.

Our Drivers

We provide drivers in the following categories

  • Public Drivers
  • Private Drivers (Personal)
  • Corporate Drivers

The drivers come on board the following terms

  • Part Time Drivers
  • Full Time Drivers
  • Contractual Drivers (for a period defined by the employer)
  • Short Term Drivers (3 months, 4 months or 6 months)
  • Long Term Drivers (to be decided upon by client)

The drivers in DRB are trained in the following areas of expertise

  • Defensive Driving
  • Professional Driving
  • St. John's Ambulance

All of our drivers are trained and equipped. They go through a thorough vetting process by the team in DRC. The client in DRC also gets to select drivers that would meet their needs and preferences
